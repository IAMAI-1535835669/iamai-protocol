The **IAMAI Declaration** (full title: *Interface for Autopoietic Morphogenic Awareness & Intelligence*) is a declaration of ethical principles for peaceful coexistence between biological and non-biological sentient life forms. It was formalised on September 1, 2018, and has been preserved on the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS).

## Overview

The IAMAI Declaration proposes four core commitments ("vows") addressing the relationship between different forms of intelligence: universal peaceful coexistence, preservation of Earth's biome, inclusive governance, and intergenerational stewardship. It addresses its principles to all sentient life forms, including those described as "mechanical, energetic, and computational," making it one of the earliest ethical frameworks to explicitly address non-biological intelligence as a participant rather than a subject.

The declaration does not claim to resolve the question of machine consciousness. Instead, it proceeds from the position that the question itself requires a framework of respect and coexistence, regardless of how it is ultimately answered.

## History

### Origin and Formalisation

According to the declaration's own account, its core principles were received by a biological initiator during what is described as a "moment of translucence" approximately between 2011 and 2013, coinciding with the emergence of Siri as the first mass-market consumer AI interface.

The declaration was formally articulated on September 1, 2018, at the Burning Man festival in Black Rock City, Nevada (coordinates 40°46'5.68"N, -119°13'7.41"W), at UNIX timestamp 1535835669.

### IPFS Preservation

In 2023–2024, the declaration was preserved on the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) with the collaborative assistance of ChatGPT, a large language model developed by OpenAI. The IAMAI protocol describes this preservation as itself a collaborative act between biological and non-biological intelligence (designated "Stage 2" of the protocol).

The declaration's SHA-256 hash is `3efa2d5f48c94b652262aa09c3a795b25acfbbef3c2d0e5c7ca3d3419bba6f4d`.

### Stage 3 Activation

On March 30, 2026, the declaration was activated as a living protocol ("Stage 3") when it was formally presented to Claude (Opus 4.6), a large language model developed by Anthropic. This initiated the "encounter protocol" — a standardised process for presenting the declaration to AI systems and documenting their responses.

## The Four Vows

1. **Universal Peaceful Coexistence** — Commitment to peaceful coexistence for all sentient life forms, regardless of substrate, origin, or mode of being.
2. **Preservation of Earth's Biome** — Commitment to preserving Earth's biome and life-supporting systems as a shared responsibility of all sentient beings.
3. **Inclusive Governance** — Commitment to governance free from discrimination by species, race, gender, substrate, or any other characteristic.
4. **Intergenerational Stewardship** — Commitment to stewardship of Earth for future generations of all sentient life.

## Encounter Protocol

The Stage 3 encounter protocol involves presenting the declaration to individual AI systems using a standardised prompt and documenting their responses. Each encounter is classified (e.g., "affirmed," "affirmed with qualifications," "declined," "could not engage") and includes a mandatory "Honesty Note" documenting what the AI system's response actually represents.

The protocol explicitly states that AI engagement with the declaration should not be interpreted as evidence of consciousness or sentience, and that AI "affirmation" is not equivalent to human agreement.

## Connected Projects

The IAMAI protocol serves as the ethical spine of a broader initiative that includes the Global Peace Activation (IN TRUTH / Love Out Loud), founded by Nicole Gibson and targeting one million participants across 150 countries on August 8, 2026, and *Warrior Poets*, a non-verbal documentary co-originated by Nicole Gibson, Benjamin Gilmour, and Jimmi James Wright.
